About Us

Welcome to Barista in House, where we believe in the power of great coffee to bring people together. Our journey began with a passion for quality coffee and a desire to create a warm and welcoming space for our community. At Barista in House, we pride ourselves on sourcing the finest coffee beans, crafting delicious beverages, and providing exceptional service to our customers. But more than just a coffee shop, we are a place where friendships are formed, ideas are shared, and memories are made.

Our Specialties

Espresso

Rich, bold, and flavorful, our espresso will awaken your senses.

Latte

Smooth and creamy, our lattes are the perfect indulgence.

Cappuccino

Frothy and delicious, our cappuccinos will delight your taste buds.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water to create a milder coffee.

Mocha

Espresso with steamed milk and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream.

Macchiato

Espresso with a small amount of milk, usually foamed, added.
